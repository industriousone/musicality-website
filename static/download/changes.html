Version 3.0 (17 Nov 2015)

Musicality 3 and Musicality Remote for iOS are now available to everyone!

You can get Musicality Remote from the App Store. It works with the iPhone, iPad, and iPod (no Apple Watch support—yet) and is a free add-on for the desktop version of Musicality.

This momentous occasion marks the ends the Musicality 3 beta. Many thanks to all of you who purchased Musicality, and to those who provided feedback and bug reports during the beta. You rock! Keep it up, and I will keep on making Musicality better.

Thanks again everyone! Enjoy!
